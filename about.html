About Claystone Tutoring

Our mission is to contribute to the learning and knowledge of our clients in the humanities, sciences, philosophy, and the process of studying itself.

Our Story

Claystone Tutoring was launched in 2024 with the intention of uplifting the community of learners, students, and scholars through offering valuable information and services in the humanities, sciences, and philosophy.

The Founder

The founder, Robert, comes from a background of intellectual inquiry and exploration. He had a deep desire to share his experience and knowledge in both these subjects and in the activity of studying itself. Having graduated from Cornell University with a B.A. in philosophy in 2020, Robert knows what it takes to succeed in higher education.

Our Mission

Claystone Tutoring wants to help you achieve your highest potential. That is its mission.
